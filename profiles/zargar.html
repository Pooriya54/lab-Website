Biography

A young Aerodynamics Engineer with extensive experience in the design and analysis of fluid flows in the aerospace and automotive industries. Skilled in advanced modeling and simulation, as well as conducting wind tunnel experiments to optimize aerodynamic performance. Proficient in specialized software and simulation tools to develop innovative engineering solutions. Passionate about flow physics and modern analytical approaches. Strong team player with the ability to manage multidisciplinary projects effectively. Committed to enhancing product efficiency and safety through advanced technologies and the latest scientific developments. Eager to learn, highly adaptable, and quick to absorb new knowledge.

  • Aerodynamic Design and Optimization
  • External Vehicle Aerodynamics
  • Wind Tunnel Testing and Experimental Aerodynamics
  • Flow Control Techniques

  • Noise, vibration, and harshness
  • Interior Flow simulation and HVAC design
