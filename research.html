1. Experimental Aerodynamics

We conduct extensive wind tunnel testing to study flow behavior over complex geometries, focusing on lift enhancement and drag reduction techniques.

2. Flow Control & Visualization

Using advanced laser-based techniques and surface flow visualization, we investigate methods to manipulate boundary layers and reduce turbulence.

3. Instrumentation Development

A key focus of our lab is designing custom sensors and data acquisition systems for high-fidelity aerodynamic measurements.
